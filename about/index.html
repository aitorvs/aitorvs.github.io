About

Aug 28, 2020
2 min read

Hi,

I am Aitor, and I am a Senior Software Engineer currently doing mobile.

I started my career as a lecturer at the University of Alcala and developing real-time operating systems and software for embedded systems, mainly for satellites like Nanosat-01.

I was lucky enough to start working at the European Space Agency, where I stayed for 10 years in several positions, from Electrical and Software Engineer to more managerial positions. After a long run I pivoted my career towards mobile development, first in ING where I led the mobile team for wholesale banking and then to Uber where my tenure is divided in product and platform teams. As part of the product team I was involved in the development of the payments framework, launched several exciting projects around cash payments, 3-domain secure (3DS) contributing to all Uber core apps.

Later I moved to a platform team where I was part of the team that developed the cros-patform framework for mobile and web development that today powers many of the core Uber features.

I am big on mentorship and a huge advocate of Kotlin and RxJava and can’t get rid of my interest on low-level “stuff”.

Throughout my career I’ve been very lucky to work with brilliant and inspiring people, on challenging projects, solving unique problems. This is my personal blog where I will share my thoughts and learnings. I often also post on Medium.
